SnailMiner About

What is SnailMiner?

We support GPU & CPU mining, although note that you can only use one at a time.

With our mining app, you can earn up to 3.5 SnailCoins per 15 minutes!

SnailMiner allows you to mine Monero and in return receive a SnailCoin depending on your hashrate. You get rewarded every 15 mins and the data gets saved on our servers.

  • CPU : Mines Monero with your CPU with xmrig
  • GPU : MInes Monero with your GPU with lolminer

Downloading and using the miner

All files can be downloaded on https://github.com/snaildos/SnailMiner/releases/

When downloading the exe, we automatically download curl, curl certs, xmrig, or lolminer depending on what version you downloaded and save into a folder called “xmrig” or, again, “lolminer”.

Introduction to MessageBackupBot

The MessageBackupBot, is the all new discord bot, allowing guild messages to be backed up, and restored. In this documentary, it shows you on how to set it up, and how to use it.

Installation

To start using the bot, you need to invite it, by clicking here.

Then, you want to select the “invite” link, and press “Invite To Your Server”. Please make sure you give it administrator or at least:

  • Manage Webhooks
  • Manage Emojis
  • Send Messages
  • Read Messages
  • Manage Server
  • Manage Channels
  • Send Embeds
  • Upload Files
  • Send Images

Without these permissions, the bot will fail to work as expected.

Limitations

The Message Backup Bot has some limitations.

  • You can save 6 channels worth up too 400 Messages (Free) [If you want more, please, view the “Upgrades” section of the wiki]
  • Restoring may take a while
  • The “bot” tag shows next to your name.

A guide too exporting your backups

It's 29 days past, and your running out of time? How do I save my backups forever? With the all new exporting feature of course!

Follow this exact guide to learn how to export.

  1. Have your backup created.
  2. Have your encryption key and backup id saves or written somewhere.
  3. Type m#export [backup id]then, go to your DMs, and enter your encryption key, after a while, return to your guild, and you will see this:

4. Please press the blue “here” button and save this file somewhere safe.

5. ALSO SAVE YOUR EXPORT ID, THIS IS VITAL!

6. At anytime, you can drag and drop your text file into a discord server with the message m#import [export id]with the attachment (obviously a guild with MessageBackupBot in it) and you will then have your content uploaded to our servers.
